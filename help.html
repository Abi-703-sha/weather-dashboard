Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get support for the Weather Dashboard

How to Use the Dashboard

The Weather Dashboard is designed to be simple and intuitive. Here's how to get started:

  • Enter a city name in the search box on the dashboard
  • Click the search button or press Enter
  • View current weather conditions and 5-day forecast
  • Add cities to your favorites for quick access
  • Check your search history to revisit previous searches

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I find weather for a specific city?
Make sure you're entering the correct city name. Try using the English name if the city is in a non-English speaking country. Some smaller towns might not be available in our database.
How do I add a city to my favorites?
After searching for a city, click the "Add to Favorites" button that appears below the weather information. You can then access your favorite cities from the Favorites page.
Why is the temperature displayed in Celsius?
The dashboard currently displays temperatures in Celsius by default. We're working on adding temperature unit preferences in a future update.
How accurate is the weather data?
Our weather data comes from reliable sources and is updated regularly. However, weather forecasting is inherently uncertain, especially for longer-term forecasts.
Is there a mobile app available?
Currently, we only offer this web-based dashboard. The website is fully responsive and works well on mobile devices.
Can I share weather information with others?
Yes, you can share the current weather view by copying the URL from your browser. The page will show the same city when opened.
